Overview

Purpose

This website will be used to inform people about white water rafting, and allow them to sign up with our company to go on a white water rafting expedition.

Audience

The type of person I think will be visiting this site is outdoors man who like nature so we will have the site be nature themed, I will be using lemon font with bold black letters. they will probably be using phones for accessing this website.
